This week at a glance

Nordic financial services and adjacent enterprise customers are clearly accelerating commitments to cloud and AI as foundational infrastructure, with Danske Bank's multiyear AWS partnership and appointment of a dedicated Head of AI signaling that these are now board-level strategic priorities rather than exploratory initiatives. The emphasis on AI enhancing both colleague productivity and customer experience points to a dual-track value case that TAMs should be prepared to articulate and support across similar retail banking clients in the region. Meanwhile, ASSA ABLOY's Capital Markets Day activity and focus on digital access and smart security solutions suggests that even hardware-oriented enterprise customers in the portfolio are pivoting toward software-defined, connected product strategies, which creates adjacent opportunities around identity, access management, and secure infrastructure. Taken together, the dominant theme is that customers across verticals are moving from digital transformation as aspiration to structured, multi-year execution with named leadership, vendor commitments, and investor-facing narratives, meaning TAMs should be engaging at the strategic program level rather than the individual workload level.
